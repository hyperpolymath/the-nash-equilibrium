== Campaign Overview

*Project:* The Nash Equilibrium — A 6X Board Game

*Funding Goal:* GBP 10,000-20,000 (500-1,000 copies initial run)
*Campaign Duration:* 30 days

== Pitch

_"What if you could play through human history — and change the ending?"_

The Nash Equilibrium is a 6X strategy board game where you guide a civilisation
from agrarian roots to post-scarcity futures. But there's a catch: Earth has
limits. Nine planetary boundaries define the safe operating space for humanity,
and every factory you build, every forest you clear, every technology you pursue
pushes those boundaries closer to collapse.

Will you exploit your way to dominance? Cooperate for collective survival? Bet
everything on a technological breakthrough? The winning strategy depends on what
everyone else is doing — that's the Nash Equilibrium.

== Risks and Mitigation

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| Risk | Mitigation

| *Complexity barrier*
| Start with 1-era tutorial scenario; layered rulebook; quick-start guide

| *Low Kickstarter interest*
| Partner with educators; pre-build mailing list; strong prototype demo

| *Manufacturing delays*
| Use reputable printers with track record; build 2-month buffer into timeline

| *Gameplay balance issues*
| Extensive playtesting before launch; commit to post-launch errata/FAQ

| *Scope creep*
| Lock base game scope before campaign; expansions as stretch goals only
|===
